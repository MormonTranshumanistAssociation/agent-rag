54             REDEMPTION OF ZION.

Though scattered and driven asunder,<br />
As exiles and pilgrims to wander,<br />
A scene on which angels do ponder,<br />
    Yet Jesus will bring their release.

When empires of Babel shall tumble,<br />
Their fabrics in ashes shall crumble,<br />
The Lord will provide for the humble<br />
    A city of refuge and peace.

There, there the Lord will deliver<br />
The soul of each faithful believer,<br />
And save them forever and ever,<br />
    And sorrow and sighing shall cease.

The saints for those blessings aspire,<br />
And wait with exceeding desire,<br />
Till earth shall be cleansed by fire,<br />
    And they their inheritance gain.

Hosanna, such blessings inspire<br />
A song from the heavenly choir,<br />
They sing of the coming Messiah,<br />
    From heaven in glory to reign.
