38 HISTORIOAL

The scriptures of their plainness robbed,<br>
And mystery thrown around,

That men might sup her golden cup,<br>
And all true knowledge drown. |

Thus generations long have passed, i<br>
And age on ages rolled,

The latter day approaching fast,<br>
Its glories to unfold. |

Our fathers of the Gentile race<br>
Traversed the western main,

And found a wide extended land, |

“Of valley, hill, and plain.

This land was peopled with a race,<br>
Which long had dwelt alone,

No record nor tradition traced<br>
Their origin unknown. |

The Lord in mercy has disclosed |<br>
The truth so long concealed,

The record found beneath the ground<br>
Has glorious things revealed.
