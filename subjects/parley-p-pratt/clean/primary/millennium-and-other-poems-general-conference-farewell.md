# General Conference, Farewell

Farewell, ye servants of the Lord,<br>
To whom we oft have preach’d the word;<br>
May you improve the wisdom given,<br>
And lead ten thousand souls to heaven.

Farewell, ye saints of latter days,<br>
With whom we’ve met in prayer and praise,<br>
In whose kind hearts the truth has shone,<br>
By which we’re gathered all in one.

Farewell kind friends, whose hearts are true<br>
We can no longer stay with you;<br>
Arise—the voice of truth obey,<br>
O come and wash your sins away.

Farewell to all whose stubborn wills<br>
Bind them in chains of darkness still:<br>
Our voice no longer you shall hear,<br>
Till Jesus shall in clouds appear:

Then you shall see, and hear, and know,<br>
What you rejected here below.<br>
Though you may sink in endless pain,<br>
Yet truth eternal will remain.
