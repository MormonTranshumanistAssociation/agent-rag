# The Pilgrim

On the shores of Ontario I’m now doom’d to wander.<br>
A pilgrim in exile, a stranger I roam,<br>
While the prince and the beggar, the wise and the simple,<br>
In palace or cottage can each find a home.<br>
The foxes have holes and the birds they have nests,<br>
And all but a preacher has somewhere to rest.
