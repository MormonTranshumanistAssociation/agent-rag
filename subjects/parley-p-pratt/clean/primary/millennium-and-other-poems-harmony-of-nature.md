# Harmony of Nature

The birds their numerous notes resound,<br>
In songs of praise the earth around,<br>
Their voices and their tongues employ,<br>
In songs of freedom, love and joy.<br>

And then behold the crystal stream,<br>
With multitudes of fishes teem;<br>
In silent joy they live and move,<br>
In freedom, union, peace and love.<br>

## Second Part

The mountains high, the rivers clear,<br>
Where heaven sheds her dews and tears,<br>
In silence, or with gentle roar,<br>
The God of love and peace adore.<br>

The earth, and air, and sea, and sky,<br>
The holy spirit from on high,<br>
And angels who above do reign,<br>
Cry peace on earth, good will to men.<br>
