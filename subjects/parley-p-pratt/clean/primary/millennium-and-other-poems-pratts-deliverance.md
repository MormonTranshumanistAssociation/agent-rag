# Pratt’s Deliverance

“The captive exile hasteneth that he may be loosed.”

ISAIAH.

The chains are rent, the dungeon’s gloom<br>
No more these active limbs confine.<br>
I rise as from the dreary tomb,<br>
Where long in prison I repined.

I mount—I fly—I haste away,<br>
Buoyed, as it were, on angel’s wings;<br>
O home! O friends! O liberty!—<br>
O God of strength, thy praise I’ll sing.

Hosanna now in highest strains,<br>
Glory to God and to the Lamb,<br>
Hosanna to the king who reigns<br>
In heaven and earth—the great I Am.
