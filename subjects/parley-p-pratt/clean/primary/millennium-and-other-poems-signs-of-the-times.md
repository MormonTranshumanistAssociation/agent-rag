# Signs of the Times

WRITTEN IN PRISON.

Lift up your heads, ye scattered saints,<br>
Redemption draweth nigh;<br>
Our Saviour hears the orphans’ plaints’;<br>
The widow’s mournful cry.

The blood of those who have been slain<br>
For vengeance cries aloud:<br>
Nor shall its cries ascend in vain,<br>
For vengeance on the proud.

The signs in heaven and earth appear;<br>
And blood, and smoke, and fire;<br>
Men’s hearts are failing them for fear;<br>
Redemption’s drawing nigher.

Earthquakes are bellowing ’neath the ground,<br>
And tempests through the air;—<br>
The trumpet’s blast with fearful sound,<br>
Proclaims the alarm of war.

The saints are scattered to and fro,<br>
Through all the earth abroad;<br>
The gospel trump again to blow,<br>
And then behold their God.

Rejoice, ye servants of our God,<br>
Who to the end endure;<br>
Rejoice, for great is your reward,<br>
And your defence is sure.

Although this body should be slain<br>
By cruel, wicked hands;<br>
I’ll praise my God in higher strains,<br>
And on Mount Zion stand.

Glory to God, ye saints rejoice,<br>
And sigh and groan no more;<br>
But listen to the spirit’s voice;<br>
Redemption’s at the door.
